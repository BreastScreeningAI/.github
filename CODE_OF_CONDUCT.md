# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

BreastScreening-AI repositories should support respectful, evidence-minded, and
privacy-conscious collaboration.

Expected behavior includes:

- Using welcoming and professional language.
- Respecting different clinical, research, engineering, and patient
  perspectives.
- Giving constructive feedback focused on the work.
- Being clear about uncertainty, limitations, and validation needs.
- Protecting privacy and avoiding patient-identifiable information.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Uploading patient-identifiable data, credentials, private datasets, or
  confidential clinical material.
- Presenting research or prototype materials as clinically validated tools when
  they are not.

## Scope

This code applies to organization repositories, issues, pull requests,
discussions, and related project communication spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers of the relevant repository. For
security, privacy, or data exposure concerns, follow `SECURITY.md`.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when needed to protect the project and its contributors.
